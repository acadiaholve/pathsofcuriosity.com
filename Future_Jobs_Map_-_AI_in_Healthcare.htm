AI in Healthcare
Future Jobs Map

Careers where humans and AI work together to improve health  ·  For students navigating what's next
Student-Created Podcast pathsofcuriosity.com

AI isn't replacing healthcare workers — it's changing what they do. The most in-demand roles will belong to people who can think across data, people, ethics, and systems simultaneously. This map shows where those careers live, what they require, and how to start exploring them now.

How to Start — Pick Your Spark

If you love people + science

Volunteer in a clinic or hospital. Shadow a physician or NP. Take anatomy and biology. Consider pre-med with a data science minor.

If you love data + coding

Learn Python and basic ML. Build a project with a public health dataset (CDC, Kaggle, UCI). Read about algorithmic bias in healthcare.

If you love ethics + policy

Study AI fairness case studies. Explore public health or epidemiology. Write about a real-world tech ethics problem you care about.

If you love communicating ideas

Write health explainers for a student audience. Create a podcast or YouTube series. Practice translating complex topics simply and accurately.
